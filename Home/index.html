Peanut

'Peanut' is more than just a name; it embodies a unique blend of coffee's warmth and the exquisite taste of carefully selected peanuts. With desserts infused with the essence of peanuts in every item on the menu, at 'Peanut', you'll experience a distinctive coffee-drinking journey unlike any other.

Nutty Delight Supreme Cheesecake

The "Nutty Delight Supreme Cheesecake" is a luxurious dessert featuring creamy peanut butter filling, a crunchy peanut crust, and a sprinkling of chopped peanuts on top. It offers a perfect balance of nutty flavors and delightful textures, making it a decadent treat for any occasion.

Crunchy Peanut Hazelnut Latte

The "Crunchy Peanut Hazelnut Latte" is a delicious coffee beverage that combines freshly brewed espresso with sweet hazelnut flavoring and a topping of crunchy chopped peanuts. This concoction offers a perfect blend of rich coffee, sweet nuttiness, and delightful texture, making it a unique and indulgent treat for coffee lovers.

Creamy Dream Peanut Butter

"Creamy Dream Peanut Butter" is a smooth and velvety spread with a rich and balanced flavor profile. Made from high-quality peanuts and crafted with attention to detail, it offers versatility in its use, whether as a spread, dip, or ingredient in recipes.
